# Product Definition: World Regional Geography Interactive Textbook

## 1. Initial Concept
An interactive, Open Educational Resource (OER) textbook for World Regional Geography, designed to provide university students with a multimodal, engaging, and pedagogically sound exploration of global regions.

## 2. Target Audience
- **Primary**: University students enrolled in World Regional Geography courses.
- **Specific Alignment**: Designed to meet Texas Core Curriculum standards for Social and Behavioral Sciences.

## 3. Core Vision & Goals
- **Pedagogical Excellence**: Move beyond rote memorization to foster "Spatial Thinking" and "Geographic Inquiry."
- **Student Engagement**: Utilize interactive elements (maps, quizzes, data viz) to maintain interest and reinforce learning.
- **Local Context**: Maintain a "Local to Global" perspective by consistently connecting regional concepts to Texas-based examples.
- **Accessibility**: Ensure content is structured, clear, and follows established style guidelines (no em-dashes, consistent typography).

## 4. Key Features (The "Gold Standard" Chapter)
Each of the 12 chapters will eventually include:
- **Interactive LeafletJS Maps**: Multi-layered maps (Physical, Political, Thematic) for spatial exploration.
- **Auto-Graded Quizzes**: Embedded Knowledge Checks with immediate feedback for students.
- **Data Visualizations**: Interactive graphs and charts for regional demographics and economic trends.
- **Biographical Highlights**: "Important Person" boxes featuring regional figures with Wikimedia Commons imagery.
- **Texas Connection Sidebars**: Specific call-outs linking the region's geography to Texas.
- **Case Studies**: Deep dives into regional conflicts, sustainability successes, or cultural shifts.
- **Scenario-Based Exercises**: "Regional Decisions" prompts that place students in role-playing scenarios.
- **Inquiry Prompts**: "Geographic Inquiry" sections that challenge students to analyze patterns.
- **Summary Tools**: A "Glossary of Terms" and "Summary of Big Ideas" to close every chapter.

## 5. Content Strategy
- **Reference Integration**: Systematic review of the project's `references/` folder to ensure factual depth and academic rigor.
- **Open Assets**: Prioritizing Wikimedia Commons for visual documentation and imagery.
- **Standardized Template**: Strict adherence to a universal chapter structure to ensure a predictable and professional user experience.
