Physical Geography

The Andes: The world's longest continental mountain range runs 7,000 km along South America's western edge.

The Amazon Basin: The world's largest rainforest and river system, containing 20% of Earth's fresh water.

The Pampas: Fertile grasslands of Argentina and Uruguay, important for agriculture.

üåê Global Theme: Biodiversity and Inequality

Latin America contains extraordinary biodiversity but also the world's highest income inequality. Deforestation in the Amazon threatens global climate while development pressures intensify.

Texas Connection

The Texas-Mexico Borderland

Texas shares 1,254 miles of border with Mexico, creating a unique binational region. Trade, migration, and cultural exchange flow across this boundary daily, making Texas deeply connected to Latin America.
