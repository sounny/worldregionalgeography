# Discussion Assignments

This folder contains weekly graded discussion prompts for Canvas. Discussions encourage critical thinking and peer learning.

## Discussion Format

- **Initial Post**: Due Thursday at 11:59 PM (minimum 200 words)
- **Responses**: 2 responses to classmates due Sunday at 11:59 PM (minimum 100 words each)
- **Points**: 10 points per discussion (5 initial post, 5 responses)

## Grading Rubric

| Criteria         | Excellent (5)                                                                                      | Good (4)                                                            | Satisfactory (3)                                                            | Needs Improvement (1-2)                               |
| ---------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| **Initial Post** | Thoughtful, well-developed response with clear connections to course material. Exceeds word count. | Good response with references to course material. Meets word count. | Basic response with minimal course connections. Meets minimum requirements. | Superficial or off-topic. Does not meet requirements. |
| **Responses**    | Substantive engagement with peers. Asks questions, offers insights, builds on ideas.               | Good engagement with peers. Adds to conversation.                   | Basic responses that acknowledge peers but don't extend discussion.         | Minimal effort. "I agree" type responses.             |
